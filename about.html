About Winslow, An Evening of the Eagles

East Coast’s Greatest Eagles Tribute Band

Winslow Tribute Band celebrates the iconic American band The Eagles, delivering their timeless music with precision and passion. This powerhouse ensemble recreates the live performance of The Eagles and the signature sound that made them one of the most successful artists in the 70s.

Our band members are seasoned musicians who have a deep love for the Eagles' music, striving to recreate the magic of their live performances. From 'Hotel California' to 'Take It Easy,' we bring the Eagles' greatest hits to life on stage.

Winslow's multi-lead vocalists authentically capture the soaring 5-part harmonies, precise instrumentation, and stage showmanship — performing the full Eagles catalog of greatest hits, deep cuts, and the solo work of Don Henley and Joe Walsh.

In Memoriam

Don Miller

Guitar / Vocals  —  2017–2024

Don Miller was a beloved member of the Winslow family. His passionate guitar work, warm spirit, and deep love for the Eagles' music made him an irreplaceable presence on stage and off. He is deeply missed by his bandmates and all who had the privilege of watching him play.
